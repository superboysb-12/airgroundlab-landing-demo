Critical Pain Points

Build-first and validate-later means high capital exposure, high uncertainty, and high waste.

High Decision Cost

  • Station siting and route design are multi-million infrastructure bets.
  • Wrong siting or wind-driven route deviation creates expensive rework.
  • The “build first, validate later” model amplifies financial risk.

Complex, Uncontrollable Environment

  • Urban “building canyons” generate unpredictable low-altitude turbulence.
  • Electromagnetic interference degrades communication and navigation stability.
  • Demand is highly volatile, making precise supply-demand matching difficult.

Severe Resource Waste

  • Failed infrastructure quickly turns into electronic waste.
  • Redundant construction and ineffective investment can burn millions of dollars.
  • Manual planning alone cannot keep up with dynamic urban conditions.

功能介绍

模块一 · 无人机物理仿真

基于高保真物理引擎模拟无人机在风场中的姿态、速度、载重与能耗变化， 让飞行策略在上线前完成可视化验证。

模块二 · 地图路网数据

接入真实城市路网数据，统一呈现地面车辆路径与低空航线关系， 支持跨区域、跨城市的配送网络规划与瓶颈分析。

模块三 · LLM 真实用户画像模拟

利用大语言模型模拟用户评论、作息时间、点单习惯与人群分布， 构建更接近真实需求波动的订单行为场景。

模块四 · 端到端 AI 洞察报告生成

从参数输入、模拟运行到结果对比自动完成，输出可解释的洞察报告， 帮助团队快速选出最优策略与资源配置方案。
